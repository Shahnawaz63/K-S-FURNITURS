information about wood

Advantages of oak wood

  1. Durability and Strength
    Oak is a hardwood, making it extremely strong and resistant to wear, dents, and scratches. Ideal for furniture that needs to last decades.
  2. Attractive Grain Pattern
    Oak has a beautiful, natural grain pattern that enhances the appearance of furniture and gives it a timeless, classic look.
  3. Resistance to Moisture (especially White Oak)
    White oak is known for its moisture resistance, making it suitable for both indoor and some outdoor furniture.
